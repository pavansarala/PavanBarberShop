Fring Cut

A fringe cut has a section of hair that's cut and styled over the front hairline, covering part or all of the forehead.

Crew Cut

A crew cut is a type of haircut in which the upright hair on the top of the head is cut relatively short.

Fade Cut

A fade is a shorter taper that blends or fades into the skin. A fade is the style for you.

ivy league cut

The high-class ivy league haircut is a stylish variation of the classic crew cut.The cut balances the smartness of a crew cut with the slickness of a side part

Dreadlocks

Dreadlocks are one of the most versatile hairstyles for black men. They can be worn short and vertical or long and loose.

Faux Cut

The fohawk is characterized by this same distinctive strip of longer hair, but allows for you to leave some hair on the sides.
